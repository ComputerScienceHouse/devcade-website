About the Gamejam

The Devcade team is hosting a week-long gamejam at RIT's MAGIC Center! The event is open to all RIT students, regardless of major, and will consist of working sessions, workshops, and seminars.

You're encouraged to think outside of the box with your game! Some existing Devcade games include remakes of old arcade games, while some can be best classified as art projects. All are welcome to be submitted for the gamejam.

Food will be provided for all in-person work sessions. Please fill out the below interst form if you plan on attending and have dietary restrictions.

There are $700 of prizes available to win, including an Elgato StreamDeck+, Raspberry Pi 4Bs, a Wacom Drawing Tablet, Visa Prepaid Gift Cards, and many more!

Interested in participating?

What do I need to know before attending?

If you plan on attending, please fill out the above interest form and we will reach out to you with further information! If you feel you need to reach out to us, send us an email at devcade@csh.rit.edu.

All participants are required to build their games using either the Monogame framework (C#) or the Bevy engine (Rust). We will be presenting (and recording) seminars on the fundamentals and build processes of both.
